Join the Group!

    Postdocs

    I currently do not have any funding set aside for postdoctoral appointments; however, I am interested in working with postdoc candidates to develop fellowship proposals to work with me. If you are interested in doing so, please reach out and we can discuss further!

    PhD Students

    I am excited to mentor PhD students who are interested in understanding how humans are impacting the climate and air quality, and how we can use this knowledge to improve human and planetary health to progress towards a more sustainable and equitable future. During your research with me, you can anticipate learning how to simulate the atmosphere on high performance computing systems, explore how satellite observations of the atmosphere have relevance for air pollution, and quantify the health and economic costs associated with air pollution and climate change. If you are interested in conducting full-time research at Cardiff University and working with me please send me an email! Further details about postgraduate research in the School of Earth and Environmental sciences can be found here.

    Masters and Undergraduate Students

    Please send me an email if you are interested in getting involved in research during your studies and to explore how climate change, air pollution, health, policy, and equity interact. During this work I will support you in familiarizing yourself with high performance computing, working on data analysis and visualization, and developing your written and presentation scientific communication skills. Depending on your interests, you could learn about climate and atmospheric science, earth system modeling, remote sensing, data science, machine learning, or health and environmental justice research. I look forward to working with you!

    You Belong Here!

    I believe that scientific research benefits from being inclusive of diverse perspectives and that it can only progress through continued awareness of inequality and inequity. I am committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for all who would like to join us.
