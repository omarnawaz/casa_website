CASA

Climate and Atmospheric Science for Action

Interdisciplinary research at the intersection of climate change, air pollution, health, equity, and policy.

School of Earth and Environmental Sciences, Cardiff University

About Us

Our research investigates how atmospheric composition, public health, and equity are affected by human activities and decision-making. We explore topics ranging from the influence of present-day fossil-fuel combustion to the future co-benefits of climate mitigation policy through earth-system modeling, remote sensing, and risk assessment.
